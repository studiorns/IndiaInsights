Brand Health Metrics

Brand health metrics measure consumer perceptions and attitudes toward our destination. These metrics are leading indicators that typically precede changes in actual travel behavior.

Awareness

94.50% (+0.70pp)

Awareness measures the percentage of the target audience who are aware of Abu Dhabi as a Cultural and Sea & Sun travel destination.

How It's Calculated:

Based on survey responses to questions like "Which international holiday destinations offering a combination of both cultural and sun & beach activities are you aware of?" The percentage represents those who respond positively out of the total surveyed population.

Interpretation:
  • 90%+: Excellent awareness, approaching market saturation
  • 70-90%: Strong awareness, but room for improvement
  • 50-70%: Moderate awareness, significant opportunity for growth
  • <50%: Low awareness, major opportunity but requires substantial investment
Business Implications:
  • At 94.50%, India has near-universal awareness of Abu Dhabi, indicating market saturation for basic awareness.
  • The +0.70pp year-over-year growth suggests continued improvement despite already high levels.
  • Marketing focus should shift from basic awareness to differentiation and consideration, as nearly everyone already knows about the destination.
  • Budget allocation should prioritize conversion rather than awareness-building campaigns.

Consideration

74.36% (+0.02pp)

Consideration measures the percentage of aware consumers who would consider travelling to Abu Dhabi in the next 3 years.

How It's Calculated:

Based on survey responses to questions like "Which of the below holiday destinations would you like to visit in the next 3 years?" The percentage represents those who respond positively out of those who are aware of Abu Dhabi.

Interpretation:
  • 70%+: Excellent consideration, strong position in consumer mindset
  • 50-70%: Good consideration, competitive position
  • 30-50%: Moderate consideration, significant barriers exist
  • <30%: Low consideration, major perception issues to address
Business Implications:
  • At 74.36%, consideration is excellent, indicating that nearly three-quarters of aware consumers would consider Abu Dhabi for their next vacation.
  • The +0.02pp slight increase shows a stable trend that should be maintained.
  • This suggests that while awareness is high, there is also strong conversion to consideration.
  • Marketing should focus on maintaining this high consideration level while addressing any emerging barriers.
  • The high consideration rate indicates a strong position in the Indian traveler's mindset, which should be leveraged in marketing communications.

Intent

64.60% (+1.60pp)

Intent measures the percentage of considering consumers who intend to visit Abu Dhabi in the next 12 months.

How It's Calculated:

Based on survey responses to questions like "Which of the below holiday destinations are you likely to visit in the next 12 months?" The percentage represents those who respond positively out of those who are considering Abu Dhabi.

Interpretation:
  • 50%+: Excellent intent, very strong conversion potential
  • 30-50%: Good intent, healthy conversion potential
  • 20-30%: Moderate intent, conversion challenges exist
  • <20%: Low intent, significant conversion barriers
Business Implications:
  • At 64.60%, intent is excellent, indicating that nearly two-thirds of those considering Abu Dhabi have strong intentions to visit soon.
  • The +1.60pp growth shows positive momentum at this critical stage of the funnel.
  • This suggests that conversion from consideration to intent is strong and improving, which is a very positive indicator of future visitation growth.
  • Marketing should focus on creating urgency and addressing practical barriers to booking (visa process, flight availability, etc.).
  • The high intent level with positive growth suggests that tactical, conversion-focused campaigns could yield significant results.

Market Characteristics

Market characteristics describe fundamental travel behaviors and patterns specific to the India market. These metrics help inform tactical marketing decisions and campaign timing.

Average Length of Stay

3.1 days

The average number of nights Indian travelers spend in Abu Dhabi per visit.

How It's Calculated:

Based on hotel guest data and visitor surveys (DVS), calculated as the sum of all nights stayed divided by the number of visitors.

Business Implications:
  • The 3.1-day average stay indicates a short-stay vacation market, slightly shorter than some other source markets.
  • This suggests marketing should focus on compact, high-impact itineraries and experiences that can be enjoyed within a 3-4 day timeframe.
  • Package offerings should be designed around this shorter timeframe to maximize appeal.
  • There's an opportunity to develop concentrated itineraries that showcase the most appealing attractions and experiences in Abu Dhabi.
  • Hotel partners should be encouraged to develop short-stay packages with value-added benefits for stays of 3-4 days.

Booking Window

44 days

The average time between booking and arrival for Indian travelers visiting Abu Dhabi.

How It's Calculated:

Based on booking data from hotels, flight booking engines and OTAs, calculated as the average number of days between booking confirmation and arrival date.

Business Implications:
  • The 44-day booking window is moderate, indicating a market that plans trips well in advance but not excessively so.
  • Marketing campaigns should be timed to run approximately 6-8 weeks before target travel periods.
  • This window allows for considered decision-making and comparison shopping by travelers.
  • Early booking incentives may be effective for this market.
  • The booking window suggests that both digital and traditional media can be effective, as there is sufficient time for consumers to respond to marketing messages.
Strategic Example:

For the September-October peak season, major campaign pushes should begin in July to align with the 44-day booking window. Early booking discounts offered 60+ days in advance could be particularly effective for this market, encouraging even earlier commitments.
